📊 OTT Customer Churn Analysis

📊Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn for an OTT subscription platform using Python and SQL. The objective is to identify factors contributing to customer churn, analyze customer behavior, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

The project demonstrates the complete data analytics workflow, including data extraction, data cleaning, KPI calculation, SQL querying, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and business insights.

---

 🎯Business Challenge

Customer retention is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Acquiring new customers is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones.

The company wants to identify customers who are at risk of churning, understand the major reasons behind churn, and develop data-driven strategies to improve customer retention and customer satisfaction.

---

 ❓Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to analyze customer churn by integrating subscription, customer, and support datasets using SQL and Python. The project focuses on cleaning and merging data, calculating key business KPIs, performing exploratory data analysis, visualizing churn trends, and generating actionable insights to support better business decisions.

💡 Key Insights

- Basic subscription plans experienced the highest customer churn.
- Monthly subscription customers churned more frequently than annual subscribers.
- Karnataka recorded the highest churn among all states.
- Customer churn peaked during September 2024.
- A strong positive relationship was observed between customer escalations and churn risk.
- Customers with frequent complaints were more likely to cancel their subscriptions.

---

🚀 Business Recommendations

- Improve customer support response time for customers with repeated escalations.
- Introduce retention offers for customers on monthly subscription plans.
- Monitor churn trends across different states.
- Proactively engage customers classified as High Risk.
- Reduce customer complaints through faster issue resolution.
